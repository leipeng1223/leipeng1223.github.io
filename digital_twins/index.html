数字孪生
项目简介：数字孪生是指通过集成物理反馈数据，并辅以人工智能、机器学习和软件分析，在信息化平台内建立一个数字化模拟。目前，FDUVIS有以下几个关于数字孪生的项目。智慧工厂可视探索系统项目在虚拟现实中构建智慧工厂的运行模型，并通过可视化和交互手段帮助用户探索其中的调度策略。城市时空数据交互分析项目在虚拟现实环境中通过对城市时空数据进行可视化和交互来进行故事生成与故事叙述。我们还将继续探索更多的可能性，比如数字孪生与NeRF的结合。
Project Introduction: Digital twin refers to establishing a digital simulation within an information platform by integrating physical feedback data and utilizing artificial intelligence, machine learning, and software analysis. Currently, FDUVIS has several digital twin projects, including the Smart Factory Visualization Exploration System project, which constructs a model of an intelligent factory in virtual reality and assists users in exploring scheduling strategies through visualization and interactive means, and the Urban Spatiotemporal Data Interaction Analysis project, which generates and narrates stories through visualization and interaction of urban spatiotemporal data in a virtual reality environment. We will continue to explore more possibilities, such as combining digital twins with NeRF.
