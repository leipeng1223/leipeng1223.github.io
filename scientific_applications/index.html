科学计算
项目简介：科学计算是指使用计算机和相关技术，通过数学模型和算法解决科学、工程和社会问题的过程。它是应用数学、计算机科学、统计学、物理学、化学等学科的交叉领域，为各个领域提供了一种强有力的工具。其中类脑计算模型就是代表之一，本实验室将类脑计算模型的功能性数据及结构性数据可视化，并对其及进行比较分析探索。
Project Introduction: Scientific computing refers to the process of using computers and related technologies to solve scientific, engineering, and social problems through mathematical models and algorithms. It is an interdisciplinary field that applies mathematics, computer science, statistics, physics, chemistry, and other disciplines to provide a powerful tool for various fields. Among them, the neuromorphic computing model is one of the representatives. Our laboratory visualizes the functional and structural data of the neuromorphic computing model, and explores and analyzes it by comparison.
