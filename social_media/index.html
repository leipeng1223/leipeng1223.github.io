社交媒体
项目简介：社交媒体可视分析是实验室主要研究方向之一，近年来在可视化和人机交互顶级会议（IEEE VIS、ACM CSCW）和期刊（IEEE TVCG等）发表十余篇论文。例如，E-Map首次提出了地图隐喻的可视化方法分析社交媒体。Co-Bridges提出一种新颖文本可视化比较方法揭示两个社交媒体数据流之间的相似点和不同点。ContextWing提出了首个针对时变上下文序列的成对比较可视分析方法。
Project Introduction: Social media visual analytics is one of the main research directions of the laboratory. In recent years, more than ten papers have been published in top conferences (IEEE VIS, ACM CSCW) and journals (IEEE TVCG, etc.) on visualization and human-computer interaction. For example, E-Map is the first to propose a visual approach of map metaphor to analyze social media. Co-Bridges proposed a novel visual text comparison method to reveal the similarities and differences between two social media data streams. ContextWing proposes the first visual analytics method for pairwise comparisons of time-varying context sequences.
