金融科技
项目简介：金融科技是一种利用计算机技术等技术手段的应用领域，旨在为金融服务提供商和消费者提供更高效、更优质、更安全、更便捷的金融服务和产品。随着金融科技的快速发展，金融业正在经历数字化、智能化和服务化转型。FDU-VIS致力于使用可视化分析技术来提高智能决策的准确性。例如，我们还通过区块链量化交易行为的可视化分析，对区块链上的高频交易策略进行深入分析;我们运用知识图谱驱动的智能投研可视化分析，帮助银行做出更智能化的投资决策。
Project Introduction: Financial technology is an application field that utilizes computer technology to offer consumers and financial service providers more effective, superior, secure, and practical financial services and products. With the rapid development of Fintech, the financial industry is experiencing a digital, intelligent, and service-oriented transformation. FDU-VIS is committed to using visualization and analytics technologies to enhance the accuracy of intelligent decision-making. For instance, we conduct an in-depth analysis of high-frequency trading strategies on the blockchain through visualization analysis of quantitative trading behavior and knowledge graph-driven intelligent investment research visualization analysis to help banks make more intelligent investment decisions.
